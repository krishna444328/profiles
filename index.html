About Me

Hi there, welcome to my website! I'm Krishna Rathore, a backend web developer based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, with over 2 years of experience in building and maintaining web applications.

I specialize in backend development and love working with technologies like Python, PHP, JavaScript, MySQL, and of course, the building blocks of the web — HTML and CSS. I enjoy creating efficient backend systems, managing databases, and connecting all the dots that make modern web applications work smoothly.

This website is my personal blog, where I share insights, tutorials, and thoughts on web development — especially backend-related topics that I find useful or exciting. My goal is to keep learning and help others grow by sharing what I discover along the way. Thanks for stopping by and getting to know me! If you enjoy my content and want to stay updated with the latest in web development, feel free to subscribe to my newsletter. I’d also love to hear from you — whether it's feedback, questions, or just to say hi!
